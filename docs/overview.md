# 🎁 Kraafto — Project Overview

> **Premium eCommerce Platform for Gifting & Luxury Crafts**

---

## Brand Identity

**Kraafto** is a premium online destination for handcrafted luxury gifts — designer lamps, glass decor, and artistic handmade items. The platform emphasizes **craftsmanship**, **gifting emotions**, and **luxury appeal**.

## Vision

Create a premium online shopping experience that:
- Emphasizes the artistry and craftsmanship behind every product
- Makes gift-giving an emotional, seamless experience
- Maximizes conversions through an immersive, distraction-free UI
- Builds lasting brand value through visual excellence

## Target Audience

| Segment | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Gift Shoppers** | People buying for birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, corporate events |
| **Luxury Decor Buyers** | Customers seeking premium, handcrafted home decor |
| **Corporate Clients** | Businesses looking for bulk/corporate gifting solutions |
| **Art Enthusiasts** | Collectors of designer lamps, glass art, and handmade pieces |

## Product Categories

- 🏮 **Designer Lamps** — Artistic, handcrafted lighting
- 🥂 **Glass Decor** — Premium glassware and decorative pieces
- 🎨 **Handmade Art** — Unique artistic items
- 🎁 **Gift Sets** — Curated gift collections for occasions
- ✨ **Limited Editions** — Exclusive, numbered craft pieces

## Key Differentiators

1. **Gifting-First Experience** — "Send as Gift" with custom messages, wrapping, and occasion tags
2. **Storytelling** — Each product tells a story of its craftsmanship, materials, and inspiration
3. **Premium Aesthetic** — High-end lifestyle brand feel, not a crowded marketplace
4. **Handcrafted Badges** — Limited edition and handcrafted authenticity indicators
5. **Occasion-Based Discovery** — Browse by occasion (Birthday, Anniversary, Corporate, etc.)
